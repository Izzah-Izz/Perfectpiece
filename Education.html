My Education

Primary School

"I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Buntal for my primary education. I transferred to this school when I was seven years old, and it became a significant part of my childhood. The transition to Sekolah Kebangsaan Buntal marked a new chapter in my early education, where I adapted to a new environment and made new friends."

Secondary School

"I attended Sekolah Seni Malaysia Sarawak (SSEMS) for my secondary education. I enrolled in this school at the age of 13, where I continued to build upon the foundation laid during my primary education.I engaged in various academic and extracurricular activities, forming lasting friendships and gaining valuable experiences that shaped my personal and educational growth."

Institution

After completing secondary school, I moved on to pursue higher education at Institusi Pendidikan Guru. The institution provided me with numerous opportunities to participate in research projects, internships, and professional networking, all of which enriched my academic experience and prepared me for my future career."
